About Us

Medtracka is a women-centric digital health solution that leverages mobile technology, artificial intelligence, and community partnerships to improve access to sexual and reproductive healthcare for women and girls in underserved communities across Nigeria. We focus on discreet contraceptive access, digital health education, AI-powered consultations and peer support groups, ensuring that women, especially young adults, vulnerable women , and those in rural areas, can access the care and knowledge they need privately and safely. Our work is supported by leading organizations like UNFPA, WorldSkills, Organon, HP Foundation, and MIT Solve, among others.

Our Mission

To transform women’s health by using technology to deliver life-saving sexual and reproductive health products, services, and education directly to women and girls especially those in under-resourced, hard-to-reach communities.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading women-centric e-pharmacy and digital SRHR platform, ensuring that every woman regardless of location, has access to trusted, affordable, and stigma-free healthcare solutions.

Our Solutions

Women-Centric E-Pharmacy (1,000+ SMRH Medications )

Our platform features a dedicated e-pharmacy offering over 1,000 verified medications, with a special focus on women’s health. From birth control pills and prenatal supplements to menstrual care products and chronic condition medications, we ensure that women can access the medications they need, conveniently, affordably, and privately.

01

AI-Powered Health Assistant – ADA AI

Our intelligent chatbot, “ADA”, is our typical African big sister, she offers real-time answers to common SRHR questions, recommends appropriate contraceptive options, and links users to verified healthcare professionals, all through a simple chat interface on the MEDtracka app.

02

Discreet Access to Contraceptives and SRHR Products through our last-mile logistics

Through our mobile platform and delivery service, users can order contraceptives, emergency pills, sanitary products, and more, confidentially and safely, right to their doorsteps.

03

Health Education & Digital Counseling

We provide evidence-based, culturally-sensitive digital content on sexual and reproductive health, mental wellness, and bodily autonomy. Women also have the option to book teleconsultations with trained providers directly from their phones.

04

Peer Support & Safe Communities

Medtracka for Her hosts anonymous peer groups, where young women and survivors can share experiences, find support, and learn from one another in a moderated, safe space.

05

On-the-Ground Outreaches

We collaborate with local NGOs and grassroots health workers to deliver care packages, offer on-site counseling, for vulnerable women seeking emergency support.
